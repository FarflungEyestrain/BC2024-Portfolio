About me

My name is Gavin Stedge-Endris. I am 18 years old and recently received a Core 40 high school diploma from Bloomington High School North. At graduation I received a certificate of completion for the Engineering Program at the Hoosier Hills Career Center. I have a dog named Daisy, she is a 12 year old pug mix.
